Water Pollution

  • Water pollution is a threat to the lives of water dwelling organisms and the ecosystems they support, food chains and habitats.
  • Pollution of water is as a result of several factors such as industrial effluent, agricultural effluent, sewage and plastics.
  • Contaminated water leads to various diseases such as stomach diseases, skin diseases, and contact with toxic substances.
  • There are measures that can be taken to minimize water pollution and they include; proper disposal of wastes, encouraging the use of environmentally friendly methods in farming and ensuring that laws are followed to the letter regarding water bodies.
